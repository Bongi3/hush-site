Hush

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Our Promise

Hush was built with one principle: your passwords belong to you, and only you. We don't collect, store, or transmit your unencrypted data. Period.

What Data Stays on Your Device

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and encrypted with AES-256:

  • Passwords, passphrases, and generated credentials
  • Vault items (credit cards, secure notes, identity documents, WiFi passwords)
  • TOTP authenticator secrets and codes
  • Security health scores and achievement badges
  • App settings and preferences

Cloud Sync (Premium Feature)

If you create an account and enable cloud sync, your vault data is end-to-end encrypted before leaving your device. Your encryption key is derived from your password locally — our servers only ever see encrypted blobs that we cannot read.

Authentication

If you create a Hush account, we store your email address and a securely hashed password via our authentication provider (Supabase). We never store your password in plaintext. You can also use the app without an account in local-only mode.

Network Requests

Hush makes two types of network requests:

  • Have I Been Pwned (HIBP) Breach Check: When you use the breach check feature, Hush sends only the first 5 characters of a SHA-1 hash of your password to the HIBP API. This is called k-anonymity — your full password is never transmitted.
  • Cloud Sync (if enabled): End-to-end encrypted vault data is synced via Supabase. Only encrypted data is transmitted.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use tracking pixels, cookies, or advertising identifiers
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit any user data
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that collect data
  • We do not require an account or registration

Payments

Subscriptions and purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Hush does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data beyond an optional email for account creation, this is inherently satisfied.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated date.
